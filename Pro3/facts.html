Facts about Nagpur

Geographical Center of India: Nagpur is often referred to as the "Heart of India" because it is geographically situated at the center of the country. The Zero Mile Stone, a historical monument, marks the geographical center of India and is located in Nagpur.

Orange City: Nagpur is famously known as the "Orange City" due to the high production of oranges in the region. Nagpur oranges are known for their sweet and tangy taste and are exported to various parts of the country and the world.

Festivals: Nagpur celebrates various festivals with great enthusiasm. One of the most notable festivals is the "Nagpur Orange Festival," which showcases the city's rich orange produce. Ganesh Chaturthi, Diwali, and Durga Puja are also celebrated with zeal.

Education Hub: Nagpur is home to several prestigious educational institutions, including the Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University (RTMNU) and the Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology (VNIT).

Maharajbagh Zoo: This is a popular zoological park located in Nagpur, known for housing a variety of animal species. It is a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike.
